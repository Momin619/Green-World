Modern Forset Garden

A forest garden is a type of garden that mimics nature, combining trees, shrubs, perennials, and edible plants to create a sustainable and diverse ecosystem. It Key features are:

  1. Layers: Multiple layers of vegetation, like a natural forest.
  2. Diversity: Variety of plants, including fruits, vegetables, herbs, and flowers.
  3. Perennial-focused: Long-lived plants reduce maintenance.
  4. Polycultures: Multiple plants grown together for mutual benefit.
  5. Minimal maintenance: No chemical fertilizers or pesticides.

It is perfect for:

  1. Small urban yards
  2. Rural properties
  3. Hillsides
  4. Coastal areas
  5. Areas with challenging soil conditions
